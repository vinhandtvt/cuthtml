Form SS-4 Instructions

Please review the form for accuracy. Do not sign the document if something is incorrect. Once the document is signed, we cannot guarantee any changes can be made prior to submitting to the IRS.

Line 7b
  • Complete this field with your US social security number or ITIN number.
  • If you do not have a US social security number, please write the word Foreign.
Upload File
  • Upload a copy of your government-issued photo ID (in English).
  • If you entered an ITIN on line 7b, please upload a copy of your ITIN letter.
Lines 16 & 17
  • If these fields are blank, please complete the principal business activity of your company.
Signature
  • Please sign the document with your mouse or your finger.

For any questions or changes that need to be made, please contact us at filings@delawareinc.com .
